## Problem 0 — Stress Harness Combined Adverse Model Is Flawed [URGENT — BLOCKS STRATEGY #1.2 PROMOTION]

**Status:** RESOLVED in Phase 40 — Stress harness corrected. Strategy #1.2 final decision: CONFIRMED_PROMOTED

**Description:**
The research stress test harness applies fee and slippage multipliers as flat per-trade dollar amounts
without scaling by position size (notional = size × price). This creates an artificially large combined
adverse penalty (~$30,000+) that is mathematically unreachable in real live trading.

**Impact:**
- Strategy #1.2 (P39_CAND_0551) currently has 8/15 stress passes
- Track C promotion requires 10/15 — fails by 2 passes
- Combined adverse is counted as FAIL but the model producing that result is flawed
- Until the harness is corrected, it is unknown whether Strategy #1.2 truly fails stress or not

**Root Cause:**
In the stress runner, fee/slippage are multiplied by a flat factor per trade:
`stress_pnl = trade_pnl - extra_fees` where `extra_fees = fee_rate × n_times` (not scaled by size×price).
This means large BTC position sizes and small position sizes are treated identically,
which is wrong. Real fees scale with notional: `fee = fee_rate × size × price`.

**Fix Required:**
Scale stress fees/slippage by `size × entry_price` per trade before applying multiplier.
Rerun stress on Strategy #1, #1.1, and #1.2 with corrected harness.

**Phase to address this:** Phase 40 — Stress Harness Repair and Strategy #1.2 Final Verdict

## Problem 1 — PF1.2 Teacher Entries Are Not Physically Executable (PRIMARY OPEN PROBLEM)

**Status:** OPEN — Primary research target for Phase 29.7 / 31

**Description:**
The PF1.2 teacher set contains 325 trades generating $21,684.99 over the research period.
These trades were generated by a 1h backtest engine (`build_p10_1_strategy()`).

When these same setups are tested through a true 5m event-driven engine (Phase 29.6), the result is:
- Net PnL: -$9,940.72
- Trades: 3,111
- PF: 0.64
- Teacher time/side matches: 1/325

**Root Cause Hypothesis:**
The 1h engine executes on closed 1h candles, which makes assumptions about SL/TP hit timing
that are not valid at 5m resolution. Specifically:
- The 1h engine assumes SL/TP is hit at a specific price within the candle.
- The 5m engine traces the actual path, revealing that many trades that "look profitable" at 1h
  resolution are actually stopped out at 5m resolution before reaching TP.

**Key files:**
- `reports/phase29_6_pf12_mtf_trade_log.csv` — 5m engine trace (1.7 MB)
- `reports/phase29_4_teacher_distilled_rules.csv` — 325 teacher entry rules
- `reports/phase29_5_teacher_mtf_trigger_match.csv` — trigger match audit
- `reports/phase29_6_execution_rule_recovery_audit.csv` — 31 recovered execution rules

**Required next step:**
Replay exactly the 325 teacher trades through the 5m engine with their exact timestamps,
then compare: (a) which trades survive 5m resolution, (b) which are stopped out, (c) what
ATR/SL/TP multipliers would need to change to recover the teacher result.

**Phase to address this:** Phase 29.7 — Teacher Trade Replay and Execution Feasibility Audit
